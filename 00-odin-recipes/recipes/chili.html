Classic Chili Recipe

Description

Chili is a hearty and flavorful dish typically made with ground meat, beans, tomatoes, and a variety of spices. The base usually features a combination of sautéed onions and garlic, which provides a savory foundation.

Chili can vary widely in ingredients and heat level; common spices include chili powder, cumin, and paprika, giving it a warm, robust flavor. The dish often has a rich, thick consistency, with tender meat and soft beans complementing each other.

It’s often served hot, garnished with toppings like shredded cheese, sour cream, green onions, or jalapeños. Chili is a beloved comfort food, perfect for gatherings and cold weather, evoking feelings of warmth and satisfaction. Whether enjoyed alone or with cornbread, it's a dish that brings people together.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef (or turkey)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1-2 tablespoons chili powder (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup beef or chicken broth (optional for more liquid)
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions, jalapeños

Instructions

  1. Cook the Meat:
    • In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Sauté Vegetables:
    • Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot. Cook until the onion is soft, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add Ingredients:
    • Stir in the diced tomatoes (with their juices), kidney beans, black beans, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. If you want a soupier chili, add the broth.
  4. Simmer:
    • Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for at least 20-30 minutes. The longer you simmer, the better the flavors meld.
  5. Taste and Adjust:
    • Taste the chili and adjust the seasoning as needed. You can add more chili powder for heat or cumin for depth.
  6. Serve:
    • Ladle the chili into bowls and add your favorite toppings.
