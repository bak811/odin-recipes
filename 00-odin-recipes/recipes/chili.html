Classic Chili Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground beef (or turkey)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1-2 tablespoons chili powder (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup beef or chicken broth (optional for more liquid)
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, chopped green onions, jalapeños

Instructions

  1. Cook the Meat:
      2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Sauté Vegetables:
      3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot. Cook until the onion is soft, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add Ingredients:
      4. Stir in the diced tomatoes (with their juices), kidney beans, black beans, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. If you want a soupier chili, add the broth.
  4. Simmer:
      5. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for at least 20-30 minutes. The longer you simmer, the better the flavors meld.
  5. Taste and Adjust:
      6. Taste the chili and adjust the seasoning as needed. You can add more chili powder for heat or cumin for depth.
  6. Serve:
      7. Ladle the chili into bowls and add your favorite toppings.
